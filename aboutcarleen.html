About Carleen

My business began in the wedding industry with on-location makeup, and has evolved to a Therapeutic Day Spa, offering services for facial treatments and skin care, makeup for all occasions, healthy skin/makeup and supplements, Oncology Esthetics, and body wraps and massage. Services also include unique, multi-dimensional photograrphy, offered selectively to clients that request a session with me.

I help brides achieve the look of their dreams on their wedding day, and work with everyone to enhance their natural beauty with high-quality, safe, and effective treatments and products. Bridal services include on-location wedding makeup, skin evealuations and programs, therapeutic skin treatments, and skincare and cosmetic products. I have also had the pleasure of working at International Bridal Fashion Week in New York City in Octoer 2012, applying makeup for the Wedding Wire video footage on the event! My work brings me amazing clients and I grow to love each and every one of them!

The Day Spa provides an opportunity for me to take the experience for my 20+ years as a sales and marketing manager and apply it to a local community business, all while mentoring the independent contractors and employee team members I work with.
