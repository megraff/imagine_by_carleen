Facial Club

Wish you could get a facial every month?

Now you can! With this monthly club you are guaranteed a discount, plus lots of other perks!

Level 1:

For $65 per month you can choose from any facial $80 or less, that includes the Sinus Facial, Facial with extractions, Dermaplaning, thirty minute peels, and more! Your choice! Plus you can choose from 3, 6, or 12 month terms.

Level 2:

For $85 per month you can choose from a Microcurrent Facial or a Signature Facial, Vitamin C Facial, Hydrating Facial, and more! Plus you can choose from 3, 6, or 12 month terms.

Level 3:

At $105 per month, this is the club level you want if you wish to have a full one-hour session plus a therapeutic peel each month. It includes the Environ Resurfacing Facial or Problematic Skin Facial. Also, choose this level if you wish to have your Microcurrent Treatment with Environ Infusion "Cocktail" Infusion each month, rather than paying for the upgrade each time.

Level 4:

At $120 per month, this is the level for having "the works" on a monthly basis! The Environ Resurfacing Facial or Problematic Skin Facial AND a thirty minute microcurrent treatment -- this is about a 75-80 minute session. These treatments are $150 when scheduled outside of the membership pricing.

All level members signed up for three months or more also recieve:

  • 10% off custom cosmetics
  • 20% off upgrades (add a peel or other add-on to your service)
  • Free gift when you join

All level members joining for six months or more also get:

  • 10% off a microcurrent or peel series package
  • The ability to roll one service to month 7
  • Birthday rewards

All level members joining for 12 months get all of the above PLUS:

  • 20$ off gift certificates (up to four per year)
  • The ability to gift one of their facials
  • Free brow waxes

The best part is that all Facial Club members can choose facials from either level at the Facial Club price if you wish to try something else or come more than once during the month!
