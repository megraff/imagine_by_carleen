Photography

I heard you were sort of interested in doing a shoot, but wanted to wait until you...get a new job, lose weight, grow your hair out, etc.

NOW is the time to capture your image! There is no other time but the present -- the past is gone and the future isn't guaranteed.

I would like to meet in person to learn about your accomplishments and transitions in life and hear your "Butterfly Story." Together, we can create your session, discuss your ideas, and your questions and concerns. I like to meet with my clients before scheduling a shoot to help us both prepare for this fun and fabuluous event! It helps me to get to know you and capture your inner Best Beautiful Self and work with your concerns, both during the shoot and while editing.

It's my job to make women feel good about themselves, and because I am also a makeup artist and esthetician, I work with my clients to help prepare you for the session. Everyone has fears, insecurities, etc...if you didn,t you wouldn't be human! I also work during the session to capture the essence o what makes each person uniquely beautiful.

As a photographer, I work with my clients to capture what makes them unique and enhance their natural beauty through the use of makeup and the various aspects of the shoot. Working with poses, lighting, atmosphere, and post capture I will bring a unique element to your photos and deliver the vision you have. Many of my clients find their sessions uplifting and confidence-building.

A photo should capture you at your moment in your life's journey, not at a moment at some point in the future which is undetermined, or you will never have one done!

Contact me to discuss your personal Beautiful Self photo session, and let's tell your Butterfly Story in images!
